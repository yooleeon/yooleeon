Internship and Job Interview Tips

These are some tips I have gathered during my experiences interviewing that I used and still use today. These tips are my personal guiding points that I wish someone wouldâ€™ve let me know as a freshman!

  1. Research and set up an extensive list of internship/job opportunities
  2. Map out interview process days
  3. Research and study information on company history, market trends, way of business
  4. Anticipate and prepare for common behavioral questions
  5. Prepare for technical questions on what the company is doing today, current events, etc. (varied based on industry) through networking
  6. Prepare thought provoking questions. Not questions with answers that can easily be found online.
  7. Be curious.
